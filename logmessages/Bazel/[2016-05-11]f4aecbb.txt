  Dynamic configurations: have HostTransition just clone the original options if they're already from the host.  This fixes an obscure incompatibility between Bazel's flag invocation policy infrastructure and multiple host configurations. In short, when multiple host configurations are possible, policy settings may not get consistently applied.
