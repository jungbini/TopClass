sitive resolution: we're now always using the attributes from the resolved configuration, instead of transitively resolving against the attributes of the transitive configuration. This change was necessary to mimic the behavior of `DependencyResolveContext` which only keeps track of the source configuration attributes.  This also fixes a mismatch with the default configuration selection not triggering the execution of the dependent tasks (typically a jar wouldn't be generated in case we were falling back on the `default` configuration).  +review REVIEW-6242
