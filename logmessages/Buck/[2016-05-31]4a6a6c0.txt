  machoutils absolutify_object_paths command and tests  Summary: `buck machoutils absolutify_object_paths --input binary --output unsanitized --old_compdir .//// --new_compdir /compdir/` This command will walk through all needed parts of the given Mach O binary and absolutify it's paths (allowing debuggers to pick up debug symbols). It will also unsanitize .a files and .o files that binary is referencing to (by fixing their comp dirs) It will perform a copy of all object files into buck-out/bin/ prior to desanitization to make sure original buck outputs are not affected.
