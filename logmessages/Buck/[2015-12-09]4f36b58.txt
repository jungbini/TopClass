  Pass Locale to TestCaseSummary and TestResultFormatter  Summary: This should be the last of the fixes to ensure our tests can control the locale under which they run.  This passes `Locale` down to `TestCaseSummary` and `TestResultFormatter` so they no longer implicitly use the system locale.  That allows us to ensure our tests pass when run in a non-English locale.
