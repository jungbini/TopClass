  Pass down all immediate linker options instead of just frameworks  Summary: System libraries required by shared libraries directly (as opposed to via dependent libraries) were not being properly passed down, leading to unresolved symbols when building libraries.  This fixes that, and consolidates the trifecta of frameworks, libraries, and linker args that's coming from the shared library itself into a `NativeLinkableInput`, and treated the same as the ones retrieved from the dependent libraries.
