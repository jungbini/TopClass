  Fixed manifest merging when activity-alias are used.  Summary: If `activity-alias` is used in application's AndroidManifest.xml and activity mentioned in 'android:targetActivity' comes from library manifest, application will fail to install.  The reason for this is that `activity-alias` requires target activity to be declared before `activity-alias` element.
