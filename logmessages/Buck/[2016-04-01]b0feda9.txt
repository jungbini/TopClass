  Ignore EEXIST when creating symlink from launch.trace -> launch.[date].[buildid].trace  Summary:This fixes a race condition when running multiple buck commands concurrently.  If they both try to create a symlink from `launch.trace` to `launch.[date].[buildid].trace` at the same time, it would previously fail with `EEXIST`.  Now, we'll just let the more recent one win.
