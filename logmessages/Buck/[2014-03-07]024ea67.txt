  Support scenario-driven primary dex packing (part 4)  Summary: This enables properties in android_binary() to allow specifying a list of classes generated based on some scenario, such that those classes and their first-order dependencies are included in the primary dex.  This is part of a series of changes to support scenario-guided optimization of which classes to include in the primary dex.
