  DirCache to keep all temp files in temp folder so it is not involved in clean up process  Summary:Previously, dir cache held all tmp files in the root of the cache folder. During clean up, which could happen on any thread, it could attempt to clean the temp file. Because of racing between cleaning thread and thread that owns that temp file, temp file is likely to moved into the place by the moment when clean operation will start. Cleaning thread could attempt to delete temp file, but it won't be there (it will be moved already), and that will procude NoSuchFile exception.
